Meeting English

Posted on 六 28 2月 2015 in English

  • Learning minutes from Austrialia Business English

Sentences used in meeting

start

  • Let's make a start
  • Can someone volunteer to take the minutes
  • Ivan has got back from hometown, so he's been out of loop on this issue
  • Jun will bring us up to scratch on what he know about the issue
  • Then we'll work out what needs to be done and who'll do what
  • Nigel would you like to start by filling us in on what you know about Walt's intentions
  • We're here to discuss the new solution proposal.
  • The purpose of the meeting is to establish a timeline for the integration testing.

consult

  • Do you have anything to add, Josh?
  • I'm out of the loop on that issue
  • I haven't heard about that, could you fill me in?
  • Could you bring me up to scratch on the project

interrupt

  • Excuse me, may I interrupt
  • Could I say something here
  • Could I just come in here
  • Could I just comment on that

Conclusion

  • So you are saying that ...
